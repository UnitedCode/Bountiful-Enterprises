About

Humble Beginnings to Becoming Excellent!
We started out as a no-name startup in the fall of 1999 working out of a small 25x50 shop. No office and no real tools to our name. 2-years later we built a slightly larger shop at 50x100 and in the summer of 2001 we moved in. Today, we have over 48,000 sqft of shop; we have provided the structural steel for over 400 projects, to 29 different states. We have developed a name that the construction industry knows and trust. Thanks to our outstanding team who will work tirelessly to maintain quality standards and ensure schedules are met. We are truly proud of the steel fabricator we have become. If you have worked with us before, you've seen the commitment from Tina during the estimating to give fair and solid pricing. As well as the ongoing efforts from Josh the Project Manager to clearly communicate with your team to ensure the steel construction goes through on time and without issues. If you are one of our suppliers or subs, you know that Mark always pays on-time or even more often pays early. The mentality of doing the very best job possible, runs through all of our departments.
